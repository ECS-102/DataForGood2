User Story D: As a community activist, I'd like to identify high needs communities who may be experiencing food shortages so that I can focus my team's work.

The FoodAccess class processes and analyzes data about countries. Complete the FoodAccess class with:

instance variables to store the data you want to analyze

a constructor to initialize the instance variables

method(s) to analyze the data to find the information needed

Possible Extensions: I would like to know what aspects make a community high risk.

Dataset: Countries and Territories
