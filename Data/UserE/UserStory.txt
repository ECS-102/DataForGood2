User Story E: As an advertising specialist, I want to identify the best basketball stadiums to advertise in so I can decide where to spend money for an upcoming campaign.

The BasketballStadiums class processes and analyzes data about basketball stadiums. Complete the BasketballStadiums class with:

instance variables to store the data you want to analyze

a constructor to initialize the instance variables

method(s) to analyze the data to find the information needed

Possible Extensions: I have been told I can advertise in up to 5 arenas.

Dataset: NBA Teams
